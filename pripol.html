Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your trust matters to us. This page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website.

Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting the LNCT Group website and reviewing our privacy policy. Our policy is simple: We collect no personal information about you unless you choose to provide that information to us. We do not give, share, sell, or transfer any personal information to a third party.

If you want to know more about how we record non-personal information about your visit or how we use information that you voluntarily submit, read on.

Non-personal Information We Record

If you do nothing during your visit but browse through the website, read pages, or download information, our website’s operating system will automatically record some general information about your visit.

During your visit, our web operating system will record:

  • The Internet domain for your Internet service, such as “xyz.com” or “xyz.net”.
  • The type of browser you are using (e.g., Chrome, Safari, Firefox).
  • The type of operating system that you use (e.g., Windows, MacOS, Linux).
  • The date and time you visit our site, and the web pages that you visit.
  • The address of the previous website you were visiting, if you linked to us from another site.

This information is used only for statistical analysis and website improvement. It does not identify individuals.

Cookies

On certain LNCT Group web pages, we use “cookies” to help you use our website interactively. A cookie is a small file stored on your computer to remember your activity and preferences temporarily.

Cookies used by our website do not collect personal information. They only help track your browser “session” to provide smoother functionality. You can adjust your browser settings to prompt, reject, or delete cookies if concerned.

Information from E-mails You Send to Us

If you send us an email, it will include your return email address. If you include personal information, it may be used to respond to your message. However, email is not always secure—please avoid sending sensitive personal data via email.

Information Collected from Interactive Forms

Some pages on our site contain interactive forms where you can voluntarily provide personal information (e.g., name, email, phone number) when registering for events, downloading forms, or attending sessions. This information is used solely for the intended purpose and is never shared with third parties.

Links to Other Sites

The LNCT website may contain links to external sites. Once you leave our website, our privacy policy no longer applies. We advise you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Downloading

Unauthorized downloading, editing, or reuse of content, images, PDFs, or documents from this website—especially for use in AI software—is a violation of Indian intellectual property law. Such actions may lead to legal consequences, including imprisonment and fines under the Copyright Act, 1957.
